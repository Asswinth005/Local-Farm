Who we are

More than a
farm share

“We didn’t set out to build a business. We wanted to rebuild the relationship between families and the land that feeds them.”

— Founders, Local Farm Co-op

Local Farm started in 2012 as a small experiment — three family farms pooling harvests to serve 40 households in Willamette Valley. Today, twenty-eight certified organic growers serve over 600 member families across Oregon.

Every dollar you spend goes directly to the grower. No platform take-rate, no shareholder dividends. Just soil, seeds, and the people who tend them.

What we believe

Our values

Land stewardship

We farm with the next generation in mind. Soil health and biodiversity come first, always.

Fair to farmers

Growers receive 100% of what you pay. No platform fees, no middlemen, no shareholder dividends.

Community first

Farm dinners, open days, workshops. Food is social — we build the table together.

Zero waste

All packaging composted or returned. Our target is zero landfill waste by 2027.

Radical transparency

Financial reports, soil data, and grower profiles published openly every season.

Seasonal eating

We only offer what’s in season. No imports, no forced ripening. Real food, real time.
